Why Whisky

A desktop music app that listens to you.

No ads, no autoplay traps, no algorithmic noise. Just a quiet curator and a clean interface.

AI Curator

Ask Whisky for facts about an artist, the backstory of a song, or a mood - get a curated, playable playlist back. Replies in English or Hindi.

Mood mixes

Ten moods × your language preference. Workout in Punjabi, focus in lo-fi, sleep in Hindi ambient - every tile is one tap.

Synced lyrics

Karaoke-grade timing. Lyrics auto-scroll to the line you're hearing, sourced live from the player's own clock.

Collab listeners

Spin up a 6-letter room code, share it with friends, see who's listening live. Powered by Supabase Realtime presence.

Browse offline

Save songs locally for offline browsing - covers, lyrics and metadata stay accessible even without internet.

Personal widgets

Pin Now Playing, Top Artists, Recent Plays, Stats and Curator Tips to your sidebar. Drag to reorder, remove anytime.
